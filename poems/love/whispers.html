Whispers in the Breeze

    Be free, my darling
    Be free and renounce the world
    Do not underestimate your power
    Let your wishes set you free
    Renounce the wants of society
    Liberate yourself from this
    world
    Dance and levitate
    And taint yourself in red!
    Tell me my darling
    Where has everyone taken you?
    And where has your soul
    remained?
